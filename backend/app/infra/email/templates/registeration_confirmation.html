Thank you for registering to vote

Your registration to vote in the UK has been submitted successfully.


As a UK voter, it is your responsibility to ensure your registration is up-to-date. If you have changed your name, address or nationality, you need to update your registration details accordingly.

You will only be able to vote in elections applicable to your current constituency.

Please note that it is a criminal offence to register to vote while pretending to be someone else. If you are found to be doing so, you will be prosecuted.

If you have any questions about the voting process, please contact the Official UK Voting Platform's Election Officials at election@officialukvotingplatform.com.

Thank you for your participation in the voting process.
